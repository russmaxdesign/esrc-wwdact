Credits

Work on She Speaks was generously funded by Project Deborah, the Victorian Multicultural Commission and the Australian Women’s Archives Project.

Without the imagination, inspiration and motivation of Dr Jackie King the She Speaks project would never have happened. Her backing has been crucial to its every facet. We thank her for her generosity, and her unwavering commitment to raising the voices of young Jewish women to a volume that makes them impossible to ignore.
